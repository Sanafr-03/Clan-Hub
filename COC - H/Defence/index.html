DEFENSE

WALL

Walls are great for keeping your village safe and your enemies in the line of fire.

CANNON

Cannons are great for point defense. Upgrade cannons to increase their firepower, but beware that your defensive turrets cannot shoot while being upgraded!

ARCHER TOWER

Archer Towers have longer range than Cannons, and unlike Cannons they can attack flying enemies.

MORTAR

The Mortar can mow down hordes of enemies by the splash damage from its shell. Don't let enemies get too close to it!

AIR DEFENSE

This anti-air tower is deadly against flying enemies, but can't target foes on the ground. Place it wisely to cover as much airspace as possible.

WIZARD TOWER

The Ultimate Arcane Defense! Tower Wizards cast powerful area effect spells that target both flying and ground troops.

AIR SWEEPER

Air Sweepers control the sky with strong blasts of air that push back flying enemies. Air Sweepers can only face one direction, so rotate them to maximize their effectiveness.

HIDDEN TESLA

Lay a deadly trap with the Hidden Tesla! Our Wizards have trapped a storm cloud into each of these sneaky towers. When an enemy walks or flies close enough, the tower springs up and fries it using the power of Electrickery!

BOMB TOWER

Bomb Towers bombard nearby ground troops and go up in a big BOOM when destroyed! Melee units best stand clear!

X-BOW

The X-Bow shoots mystical bolts with terrifying power. Load it with Elixir and the X-Bow works automagically. You can set it to target ground units at long ranges, or all targets at reduced range.

INFERNO TOWER

Set the Inferno Tower's Dark Elixir fueled flame to build up unbelievable damage to single targets, or to constantly roast multiple targets at once.

EAGLE ARTILLER

The Eagle Artillery has nearly unlimited range and targets tough enemies with exploding shells. However, it won't activate until a large amount of troops have been deployed.

SCATTERSHOT

The Scattershot heaves very heavy bundles of poorly tied together rocks at whoever happens to be closest. The bundles break apart on impact and deal additional damage to the troops behind.

BUILDER'S HUT

Nothing gets done around here without Builders! You can hire more Builders to start multiple construction projects, or speed up their work by using green gems.

SPELL TOWER

For years, Wizards alone brought magic to the battlefield one fireball at a time. Now they've developed a fully automated way to give your village more ways to mess up attackers with magic!

MONOLITH

The Builder's first experiment in using Dark Elixir for a building resulted in something truly frightful. The stronger the Monolith's target, the more damage it does. Great to have defending your Village, but a little scary to attack against.

MULTI ARCHER TOWER

These Archers have teamed up to take out attackers faster and more furiously than ever. Teamwork makes the dream work!

RICOCHET CANNON

Builder has used one weird trick to develop a devastating cannonball that will ricochet and hit a second target.

FIRESPITTER

The Firespitter spews flames at a fire rate faster than the X-Bow! If it misses, the Firespitter will shoot past its target to deal damage to enemies behind. It can only face a single direction, so place carefully!
