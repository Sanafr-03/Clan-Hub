BARBARIAN KING

Biggest and bravest of all barbarians, this colossal menace soaks up huge amounts of damage and smashes anything in his path with his sword. He guards his territory fiercely when defending.

EQUIPMENTS

Summons a pack of Rage Barbarian

Casts Rage on the Barbarian King

Causes a powerful earthquake which destroys Walls and damages Buildings

Every time the Barbarian King attacks, he heals himself

Barbarian King becomes gigantic, doing area damage and taking less damage

Shoots a Spiky Ball which bounces between buildings
