MAGIC ITEMS

POWER POTION

Increases troop, spell and siege machine levels to the maximum permitted by the Town Hall and Builder Hall level for 1 hour.

RESOURCE POTION

Accelerates production of resources in the Home Village by 2x for 1 day.

TRAINING POTION

Accelerates rate of training troops in the Home Village by 4x for 1 hour.

BUILDER POTION

Accelerates the work of Builders in the Home Village by 10x for 1 hour.

HERO POTION

All Heroes and Pets gets boosted to the maximum level permitted by their Town Hall or Builder Hall level for 1 hour.

RESEARCH POTION

Accelerates work inside the Laboratory by 24x for 1 hour.

SUPER POTION

Boosts a Troop to its Super Troop counterpart (if possible) for 3 days.

BOOK OF FIGHTING

Instantly finishes any ongoing Troop upgrade within the Laboratory.

BOOK OF BUILDING

Instantly finishes any ongoing building upgrade or construction.

BOOK OF SPELLS

Instantly finishes any ongoing Spell upgrade within the Laboratory.

BOOK OF HEROES

Instantly finishes any ongoing Hero or Pet upgrade. If used on Heroes, it will also regenerate both health bars.

BOOK OF EVERYTHING

Instantly finishes any ongoing upgrade timer. If used on Heroes, it regenerates both health bars.

RUNE OF GOLD

Fills all Gold Storages.

RUNE OF ELIXIR

Fills all Elixir Storages.

RUNE OF DARK ELIXIR

Fills the Dark Elixir Storage.

SHOVEL OF OBSTACLES

Makes a single obstacle permanently movable.

WALL RING

Instantly upgrades a Wall to no cost. Higher level walls will require increasing amounts of Wall Rings.

HAMMER OF FIGHTING

Upgrades any troop instantly at no cost.

HAMMER OF SPELLS

Upgrades any Spell instantly at no cost.

HAMMER OF BUILDING

Upgrades any building instantly at no cost.

HAMMER OF HEROES

Upgrades any Hero or Pet instantly at no cost. If used on a Hero, it instantly regenerates both health bars.
